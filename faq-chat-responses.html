How is the exam scored?

Each question allows for up to two correct selections. You get 1 point for each correct answer selected. If you select an incorrect answer, 0.5 points are deducted. The maximum score for a question with two correct answers is 2 points, and the minimum is 0 (scores per question do not go negative). Your total score is the sum of points from all questions.

Can I pause the exam and resume later?

Currently, this mock exam interface does not support saving progress and resuming later. If you navigate away or close the browser, your progress will likely be lost. It's best to complete the exam in one session.
